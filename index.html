ABOUT ME

My name is Martin and I’m a front-end web developer. Following over a decade working as a leader and manager in multi-million to multi-billion pound companies, working with teams ranging in size from 15 - 300+, I’ve decided I couldn’t ignore my passion any more.

Having seen the web grow first hand as a user from the early days of MSN Messenger, through Myspace and Newgrounds, to the Progressive Web applications and tech giants of today, I am passionate about being a part of building and developing this exciting world.

My love of interesting graphic-design and timeless branding, desire to solve problems with logical solutions, and overall desire to constantly build something new from the ground up have led me down this exciting new path.
